Product Designer & Low Code Developer

My name is Olaniyi Ogungbe. I am a highly skilled Product Designer and Low Code Developer with a proven track record of transforming concepts into intuitive, visually appealing digital experiences

My expertise lies in user interface design, wireframing, and prototyping, coupled with a strong knowledge of wordpress, shopify, webflow and wix for low code development. I am adept at driving product innovation and enhancing user satisfaction through a user-centric approach.
